Her Story

01

The Shy Phantom

Rice Shower moves through the world like fog — barely there, yet impossible to forget. With eyes that carry the weight of a thousand unspoken prayers, she is a girl of stillness. But step onto the track, and the phantom stirs. Something ancient and relentless rises from beneath the quiet surface.

02

Bringer of Misfortune

When she defeated the beloved Mihono Bourbon at the Kikuka Sho, the crowd turned cold. They called her a villain — a dark horse who stole dreams. Yet she did not run to be loved. She ran because the track asked her to, and turning away from that call was something she simply could not do.

03

The Spring Tiara

The Tenno Sho (Spring) changed everything. She crossed the finish line first — and this time, the silence that followed was different. Not cold, but reverent. The crowd had finally seen what was always there: a girl who ran not against them, but alongside their very longing for something pure and true.

04

A Legacy of Rain

Rice — a blessing poured from the sky. Her name holds that paradox: something that falls like sorrow but feeds like grace. Like rain that darkens the sky before nourishing the earth, her path was shadowed by misunderstanding. But the dark flower did not wilt. It bloomed eternal, for those who learned to look.
